Short (max 80 characters)

Full
Block Puzzle is a relaxing yet strategic block placement game. Drag blocks onto the board and clear full rows or columns to score. There’s no time pressure — focus on planning, combos and high scores.

Features
- Simple drag‑and‑drop gameplay, hard to master
- Clean visuals and smooth performance
- Optional rewarded videos for extra stars — fair and player‑friendly
- Play at your own pace

Contains ads. No forced videos. Rewarded ads are strictly optional and grant bonuses only after completion.
